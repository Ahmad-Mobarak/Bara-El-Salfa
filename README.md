**Bara Al-Salfa** (literally "Outside the Topic") is a web-based implementation of the popular social deduction party game. It is designed specifically for the Arab community with a polished **RTL (Right-to-Left)** interface and culturally relevant categories.

In this game, players are detectives trying to figure out who among them is "The Outsider"â€”the one person who doesn't know the secret word. The Outsider's goal? Blend in, lie, and figure out the secret word before getting caught!

## ğŸ¯ How to Play

### 1. Setup Phase
1.  **Host** selects a category (e.g., Animals) and player count on one device.
2.  Pass the device around! Each player taps to reveal their identity.
    * **Regular Players:** See the secret word (e.g., "Lion").
    * **The Outsider:** Sees "You are the Outsider" (Bara Al-Salfa).

### 2. Discussion Phase ğŸ’¬
Players take turns describing the word *without* giving it away completely.
* *Player A (Lion):* "It lives in the jungle."
* *Player B (Lion):* "It has a loud voice."
* *Outsider (Guessing):* "It... eats meat?"
